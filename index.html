Minecraft Texture Pack Guide

This website will teach you how to make a minecraft texture pack for free!

How to make it:

To make a free Minecraft texture pack, go to createtextures.com and create your textures search the texture you want to change. After you finished making the texture for your item or block press save texture and you could move on to your next texture. When you finished the pack simply download it and open minecraft go to options then resourse packs and drag your downloaded texture pack to Minecraft(java) and you're done! now you could use your own custom texture pack.
